ABOUT US

Terri Davis is the proud owner and chef of Taste the Difference Events. She built the company from a small home-based bakery, selling cakes to local supermarkets, to a full service catering and events company. Although our bread and butter are corporate catering and events, we are also skilled in the elaborate detailing necessary for private functions.

We offer corporate and residential event catering including weddings and birthdays… If You Can Dream It We Can Do It!
